1. To change lower case characters to upper case, you can

echo abc | tr "[a-z]" "[A-Z]"

or 

echo abc | tr '[:lower:]' '[:upper:]'

2. To change upper case characters to lower case, you can

echo ABC | tr "[A-Z]" "[a-z]"

or

echo ABC | tr '[:upper:]' '[:lower:]'
